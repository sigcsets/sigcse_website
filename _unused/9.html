A variety of programs that support our mission and serve our members:

  • SIGCSE Awards - Information about SIGCSE Outstanding Contribution to Computer Science Education and SIGCSE Lifetime Service to the Computer Science Education Community awards, the nomination processes, and previous award winners.
  • Test of Time Awards - Information about SIGCSE Test of Time awards and the nomination process.
  • Special Projects - The Special Projects program provides funding for small projects of interest to the SIGCSE community.
  • Speaker's Fund - The SIGCSE Speaker's Fund supports the dissemination of outstanding SIGCSE/ITiCSE/ICER presentations to other venues.
  • Travel Grants - A program to help defray the cost of attending a SIGCSE Technical Symposium.
  • Local Chapters - A description of how to create SIGCSE local chapters.
  • In-Cooperation - ACM SIGCSE will lend its name to non-SIGCSE events that are fully sponsored by other non-profit organizations.
  • SIGCSE Committees - SIGCSE's Committee initiative.
  • SISL in STEM - SIGCSE is one of the groups taking part in the Project Kaleidoscope (PKAL) project: Sustainability Improves Student Learning in STEM (SISL in STEM).
